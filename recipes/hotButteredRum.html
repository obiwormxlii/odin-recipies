Hot Buttered Rum

Description

A nice, hot, boozy drink can be a great way to cozy up on a cold winter's night. There's a few to choose from, like mulled cider, a hot toddy, old fashioned grog, or a hot buttered rum like this recipe from The Spruce Eats.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons packed brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 splash pure vanilla extract
  • 1 dash ground cinnamon
  • 1 dash ground nutmeg
  • 1 dash ground allspice
  • 2 ounces dark rum
  • 4 ounces hot water
  • Cinnamon stick, for garnish

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Place the sugar, butter, vanilla extract, and spices into the bottom of an Irish coffee glass or mug and mix well or muddle.
  3. Pour in the rum and top it with hot water.
  4. Stir and garnish with a cinnamon stick. Serve and enjoy.
