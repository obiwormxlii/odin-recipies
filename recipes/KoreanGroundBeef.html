Korean Ground Beef

Description

Korean Ground beef is one of my go-to's for a quick, easy, delicious meal. There's usually no leftovers if I have anything to say about it.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cups low sodium soy sauce or liquid aminos
  • 1/3 cups brown sugar
  • 1/4 cups beef broth
  • 2 t sesame oil
  • 1 Tbs minced garlic
  • 1 Tbs minced ginger
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper optional
  • 1 1/2 lb extra lean ground beef
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 cups rice
  • 20 oz frozen broccoli steamer
  • 1/2 cups chopped green onion
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Mix first 7 ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Start cooking the rice in a medium pot or a rice cooker
  3. Cook the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat, stirring in the garlic powder. Stir occasionally to break up chunks until cooked thoroughly
  4. Drain excess grease. Stir sauce, pour over meat and simmer for 5-10 minutes until heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cook broccoli according to package directions. Garnish with chopped green onion.
